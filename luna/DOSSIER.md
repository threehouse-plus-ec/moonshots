## Systems-Engineering Signature

- Fragmented design bureau structure (OKB-1, Lavochkin)
- Political opacity and inter-bureau competition
- Exploration-first strategy (firsts in impact, soft landing, orbiting, sample return)
- High early failure rate with gradual reliability improvement
- Luna 25 (2023): revival attempt ending in crash — continuity of systemic risk?
