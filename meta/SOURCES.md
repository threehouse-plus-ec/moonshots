## Source Classification Protocol

Per Guardian flags G-4 and G-7, all sources are tagged:

- **[P]** Primary: Original documents, mission logs, review board reports, contemporary testimony, raw data.
- **[S]** Secondary: Scholarly analysis, historical synthesis, interpretive works.
- **[T]** Tertiary: Popular accounts, media, educational materials.

**Registry rules:**

1. Every ledger entry must cite ≥1 [P] source for constraints (Section C).
2. Every ledger entry must cite ≥1 source external to NASA institutional perspective (G-7 mitigation).
3. Where [P] sources conflict, the conflict is documented here and in `COUNCIL-NOTES.md` (Sails layer), not resolved within the ledger classification.
4. Append only; never remove. Version bumps tracked in `CHANGELOG.md`.
