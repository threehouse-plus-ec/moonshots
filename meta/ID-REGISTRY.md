## Convention

- **Local alias:** `CL-APOLLO-nnn` or `CL-ARTEMIS-nnn` — used within dossier files for readability.
- **Global ID:** `CL-2026-NNN` — unique across the entire Harbour Breakwater Layer.
- Global IDs are assigned sequentially. Prior allocations: CL-2026-001 (consciousness-convergence), CL-2026-002 (topological thesis).
