## Complete Mission Table

| Mission | Status | Launch Date | Launch Vehicle | Crew | Spacecraft | Primary Objective | Outcome / Notes |
|---------|--------|-------------|----------------|------|------------|-------------------|-----------------|
| Artemis I | Completed | 16 Nov 2022 | SLS Block 1 | Uncrewed | Orion (CM-002) | Uncrewed integrated flight test of SLS and Orion; lunar orbit and return | Success. 25-day mission; Orion entered distant retrograde lunar orbit; heat shield experienced greater-than-expected erosion during re-entry [Source: NASA] |
| Artemis II | In Progress | 1 Apr 2026 | SLS Block 1 | 4 (Wiseman, Glover, Koch, Hansen) | Orion (CM-003) | First crewed Artemis flight; lunar flyby; free-return trajectory | In flight. 10-day mission; crewed lunar flyby at approximately 4,000 miles altitude; testing life support and crew systems [Source: NASA] |
| Artemis III | Planned | Mid-2027 | SLS Block 1 | TBA | Orion + HLS | Crewed test of Human Landing System in low Earth orbit; rendezvous and docking with SpaceX Starship HLS and/or Blue Origin Blue Moon | Planned. Will test HLS rendezvous, docking, and systems in LEO; evaluate AxEMU spacesuit; propellant transfer demonstration [Source: NASA] |
| Artemis IV | Planned | Early 2028 | SLS Block 1B | 4 (TBA) | Orion + HLS | First crewed lunar landing since Apollo 17; south polar region | Planned. First use of SLS Block 1B with Exploration Upper Stage; crew transfers to HLS for lunar surface mission; approximately 30-day duration [Source: NASA] |
| Artemis V | Planned | Late 2028 | SLS Block 1B | 4 (TBA) | Orion + HLS | Second lunar landing; begin construction of lunar surface base | Planned. Continued lunar surface exploration; deployment of foundational surface habitat elements; approximately 30-day duration [Source: NASA] |
| Artemis VI+ | Planned | 2029+ | SLS Block 1B | TBA | Orion + HLS | Sustained lunar exploration; annual missions | Planned. NASA anticipates approximately annual lunar landing missions following Artemis V [Source: NASA] |

## CLPS Missions (Commercial Lunar Payload Services)

CLPS missions support Artemis by delivering robotic payloads to the lunar surface via commercial providers.

| Mission | Provider | Launch Date | Status | Objective | Outcome |
|---------|----------|-------------|--------|-----------|---------|
| Peregrine Mission 1 | Astrobotic | 8 Jan 2024 | Failure | First CLPS mission; 5 NASA payloads | Propulsion anomaly prevented lunar landing; spacecraft returned to Earth atmosphere [Source: NASA] |
| IM-1 (Odysseus) | Intuitive Machines | 15 Feb 2024 | Partial success | First commercial lunar landing; 6 NASA payloads | First commercial soft landing; lander tipped on side; limited science operations [Source: NASA] |
| Blue Ghost 1 | Firefly Aerospace | 15 Feb 2025 | Success | Lunar landing; 10 NASA payloads | Successful landing; mission concluded 16 March 2025; science analysis ongoing [Source: NASA] |
| IM-2 (Athena) | Intuitive Machines | 27 Feb 2025 | Partial success | Lunar south polar landing; PRIME-1 drill | Landed in crater near south pole; lander on side; limited operations [Source: NASA] |
| IM-3 | Intuitive Machines | 2026 (planned) | Planned | Lunar landing | Planned [Source: NASA] |
| Blue Ghost 2 | Firefly Aerospace | 2026 (planned) | Planned | Lunar landing | Planned [Source: NASA] |

## Mission Architecture Summary

| Mission | Trajectory | Lunar Orbit Duration | Surface Duration | Total Duration |
|---------|------------|---------------------|------------------|----------------|
| Artemis I | Distant retrograde orbit | ~6 days | N/A | 25 days |
| Artemis II | Free-return flyby | N/A | N/A | 10 days |
| Artemis III | LEO testing | N/A | N/A | TBA |
| Artemis IV | NRHO + surface | TBD | ~6.5 days | ~30 days |
| Artemis V | NRHO + surface | TBD | ~6.5 days | ~30 days |
