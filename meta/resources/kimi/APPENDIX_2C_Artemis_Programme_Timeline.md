# Appendix 2C: Artemis Programme Timeline

**Programme:** Artemis (2017–present)  
**Document Type:** Programme Timeline  
**Date Compiled:** 2026-04-04

---

## Chronological Milestones

| Date | Event | Significance |
|------|-------|--------------|
| 11 Dec 2017 | Space Policy Directive-1 | President Trump directs NASA to return humans to the Moon, followed by missions to Mars [Source: White House] |
| Feb 2018 | Artemis Programme Initiation | NASA formally initiates lunar return programme; SLS and Orion development continues |
| 2018–2020 | SLS/Orion Development | Continued development and testing of core stage, boosters, and Orion systems |
| 5 Dec 2014 | EFT-1 Mission | Orion Exploration Flight Test-1 validates heat shield and systems [Source: NASA] |
| 2 Jul 2019 | Ascent Abort-2 Test | Launch abort system validated at maximum dynamic pressure [Source: NASA] |
| 16 Nov 2022 | Artemis I Launch | First integrated flight of SLS and Orion; uncrewed lunar orbit mission [Source: NASA] |
| 11 Dec 2022 | Artemis I Splashdown | Successful conclusion of 25-day mission; data analysis begins [Source: NASA] |
| 2023 | Heat Shield Anomaly Investigation | NASA investigates unexpected heat shield erosion during Artemis I re-entry [Source: NASA] |
| Apr 2021 | SpaceX HLS Selection | NASA selects SpaceX Starship as Artemis III HLS provider ($2.9 billion) [Source: NASA] |
| May 2023 | Blue Origin HLS Selection | NASA selects Blue Origin Blue Moon as second HLS provider (Artemis V+) [Source: NASA] |
| 1 Apr 2026 | Artemis II Launch | First crewed Artemis flight; lunar flyby with four-person crew [Source: NASA] |
| Mid-2027 | Artemis III (Planned) | Planned HLS testing in LEO; AxEMU spacesuit evaluation [Source: NASA] |
| Early 2028 | Artemis IV (Planned) | First crewed lunar landing since Apollo 17 [Source: NASA] |
| Late 2028 | Artemis V (Planned) | Second lunar landing; lunar base construction begins [Source: NASA] |

## Programme Phase Summary

| Phase | Period | Key Activities |
|-------|--------|----------------|
| **Programme Initiation** | 2017–2018 | Policy establishment, programme structure definition |
| **Infrastructure Development** | 2018–2022 | SLS/Orion integration, ground systems preparation |
| **First Flight** | 2022 | Artemis I uncrewed test mission |
| **Crewed Flight Preparation** | 2023–2026 | Artemis II preparation, heat shield resolution |

| **Lunar Landing Campaign** | 2027–2028+ | Artemis III–V lunar landing missions |
| **Sustained Operations** | 2028+ | Annual lunar landing missions |
