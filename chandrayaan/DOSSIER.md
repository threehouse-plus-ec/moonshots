## Systems-Engineering Signature

- Extreme cost optimisation (Chandrayaan-3 at ~$75M — fraction of comparable missions)
- Sharp failure → success trajectory (Chandrayaan-2 crash → Chandrayaan-3 south pole landing)
- "Failure-based design" philosophy: design against failure scenarios, not toward success path
- Same team retained post-failure (no blame culture; learning mandate)
- Indigenous technology development with selective international collaboration
- First south polar landing (August 2023)
