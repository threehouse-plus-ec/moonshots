## Mission Type Classification (Owen Maynard's Alphabetical Scheme)

| Type | Description | Missions |
|------|-------------|----------|
| A | Uncrewed Saturn V/CSM qualification | Apollo 4, Apollo 6 |
| B | Uncrewed LM qualification | Apollo 5 |
| C | Crewed Earth orbit CSM test | Apollo 7 |
| C′ | Crewed CSM lunar orbit (modified C without LM) | Apollo 8 |
| D | Crewed LM operations in Earth orbit | Apollo 9 |
| E | Crewed LM operations in lunar orbit (cancelled) | — |
| F | Crewed LM descent to near-lunar surface | Apollo 10 |
| G | First lunar landing | Apollo 11 |
| H | Precision landing; ALSEP deployment; 2 EVAs | Apollo 12, Apollo 13 (planned), Apollo 14 |
| I | (Not used) | — |
| J | Extended missions with LRV; 3 EVAs; extended stay | Apollo 15, Apollo 16, Apollo 17 |
